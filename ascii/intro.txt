    -------------  Welcome to LIRI Bot  ---------------

    Search for upcoming concerts, and information
    on your favorite songs and movies.

    To begin, type 'node liri', followed by one of
    these commands:

        o concert-this <artist or band name>
        o spotify-this-song <song title>
        o movie-this <movie title>
        o do-what-it-says

        * Example: node liri concert-this ok go
